About Us





Mission

To develop internationally aligned language curricula and implement language training programs for Filipino workforce with positive workplace values and culture

Vision

By 2028, the National Language Skills Center is an internationally recognized specialized center for language training to Filipino job-ready workers

Mandate

  • Conducts different Language Training Program to equip the job-ready Filipino workers prior to local and global employment;
  • Serves as the central language skills facility of all regional LSI's;
  • Develops Language Curricula; and
  • Becomes the center for Language Trainer's Development

Value Statement

We believe in demonstrated competence, institutional integrity, personal commitment, culture of innovativeness, and a deep sense of nationalism.

TESDA OFFICIAL SLOGAN LOGO

Through the years, TESDA has reached millions of our kababayans by providing inclusive and free technical vocational education and training (TVET) nationwide through various training modalities.

The new TESDA slogan and logo demonstrate the Agency's stronger advocacy to give our kababayans even better access to quality-assured and globally competitive tech-voc, and communicate to the public TESDA's aspiration to provide public service with a heart aligned with the administration's brand of governance and leadership ‘Bagong Pilipinas’.

Sa TESDA, Lingap Ay Maaasahan.

Source: https://www.tesda.gov.ph/

In 2007, the TESDA-National Language Skills Institute (NLSI) was established in line with the government’s recognition of the importance of language proficiency in the global labor market. TESDA-NLSI aims to respond and address the local and global industry requirements of language proficiency by providing world class workplace-based language skills training, not only for the skilled Filipino Job-ready workers but also to reach out and serve new and more clients and partners.
