National Language Skills Center


TESDA National Language Skills Institute aims to enhance language proficiency among Filipinos by offering programs in English, Spanish, and other languages. Our goal is to empower individuals with the linguistic skills essential for employment and personal growth. Through innovative teaching methods, we equip learners with effective communication abilities for diverse contexts.

TESDA is now accepting enrollees for Japanese and English Proficiency

The Japanese Language and Culture course is designed to help trainees to further strengthen their understanding of the Japanese culture including workplace standards, norms, and work ethics. It aims to equip trainees who intend to live and work in Japan or do administrative work in Japanese companies in the Philippines.

“I encourage our kababayans to consider enrolling in language training programs. Embrace the opportunity to refine your language skills, gain a global perspective, and enhance your employability,” the TESDA Secretary said.

The training duration for English Proficiency for Customer Service Workers is 100 hours or 25 days; while for Japanese Language and Culture is 300 hours or 75 days.

TESDA National Language Skills Center is dedicated to providing language training and proficiency programs. The NLSC is focused on enhancing the language skills of individuals to improve their employability and communication abilities, particularly in the context of the global workforce.

TESDA to give English Proficiency Training to Tondo Youth

MANILA – An initial 50 young people from Tondo, Manila will be given free English proficiency training by the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA), the agency said Tuesday.

TESDA Deputy Director General Aniceto Bertiz III told the Philippine News Agency that TESDA targets 500 scholars. The initial 25 will start their training on August 24, while another 25 will begin training on the first week of October.

"We have provided (an initial) 50 slots, but if our NGO (non-government organization) partner from Baseco could send us more enrollees, we could still accommodate another 50," he said.

TESDA's English Proficiency for Customer Service Workers training runs for 100 hours or 25 days, and is being implemented through full online learning. Entrants must be at least high school graduates to be considered for the program.

Programs and offers



SPANISH LANGUAGE FOR DIFFERENT VOCATIONS

100 hours / 25 days

This course is designed to enhance the KSA to perform task and showcase work ethics in accordance with the industry requirements and standards. This will also help Filipino workforce to increase their capacity and competency in terms of adopting skills and practices using the Spanish Language for Different Vocations. This program also aims to train interested individuals on Spanish language so that they can communicate using the basic Spanish Language and workplace conversational language. Upon completion of the training, graduates must be able to pass A1 Level under DELE (diplomas de Español como Lengua Extranjera) or Common European Framework of References for Languages.

ENGLISH PROFICIENCY FOR CUSTOMER SERVICE WORKERS

100 hours / 25 days

This course is designed to enhance the KSA to perform customer service and marketing in accordance with the industry requirements and standards. The Customer Service Workers cover the job of Customer Service Representatives, Telemarketers, Office Front Desk Representatives, Call Center Agents and Marketing Agents.​ This program also aims to train interested individuals on English proficiency so that they can communicate using the Basic English Language and workplace conversational language.

JAPANESE LANGUAGE AND CULTURE

100 hours / 25 days

This course is designed to enhance the KSA in reading, writing, speaking and listening in Japanese Language, constructing sentences and how to conduct simple transactions. The trainees shall be exposed to Japanese way of living, culture and its geography. This program shall be integrated with work ethics and values.

300 hours / 75 days

This course is designed to further enhance the KSA in reading, writing, speaking and listening in Japanese Language and Culture. It includes a higher level of conversation and additional kanji characters in preparation for the Japanese Language examinations. This program will help the trainees to further strengthen their understanding of the Japanese culture including workplace standards, norms and work ethics. It aims to equip trainees who intend to live and work in Japan or do administrative work in Japanese companies in the Philippines.

BASIC KOREAN LANGUAGE AND CULTURE

100 hours / 25 days

This course is intended to hone the KSA in reading, writing, speaking and listening in Korean Language. This course also covers creating own sentences and basic conversation that will be useful in daily life. This includes the familiarity of the learners to different Korean etiquette, culture and norms.

Requirements

Trainee's Entry Requirements

  • Must be a Filipino citizen
  • At least (18) years old
  • At least a High School Graduate
  • Must not be currently Enrolled

Documentary Requirements

  • PSA/NSO Birth Certificate
  • Marriage Certificate(for married women only)
  • High School or College Diploma / Transcript of Records
  • Latest id picture in white background ( 2pieces "1x1")
